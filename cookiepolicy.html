Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 November 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Rung’s Thai Kitchen (“we”, “our”, “us”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function properly and provide information to the site owners.

2. What cookies we use

At the moment, we only use cookies that are necessary to:

  • Make the website work correctly
  • Keep your session secure while you submit information (for example, when placing an order)
  • Improve the performance and security of the site

We do not currently use cookies for advertising or behavioural tracking.

3. Analytics

If we enable basic, privacy-friendly analytics in future (for example, to understand which pages are used most), we will only use it to improve our service. If we introduce marketing or third-party tracking cookies, we will update this policy and (where legally required) ask for your consent.

4. Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. If you block essential cookies, parts of the site may not work properly.

5. Contact us

For any questions about cookies, contact us at admin@rungsthaikitchen.co.uk.
